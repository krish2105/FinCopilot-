# Demo script (2-minute Loom)

Filled in after Phase 6/8. The demo must show three things:

1. **A cited answer** — ask a simple factual question (e.g. "What was Apple's revenue
   in the latest 10-K?"), show inline citation chips and the source-excerpt panel with
   a page reference, and the "Hybrid Search" route badge.
2. **A GraphRAG relationship query** — e.g. "Which subsidiaries share this risk?" —
   show the "GraphRAG" route badge and the relationship-aware answer.
3. **A refusal** — ask something the corpus can't support; show the calm, intentional
   "insufficient evidence" state explaining what evidence would be needed.

Also flash the **evaluation dashboard** (RAGAS faithfulness/relevance/precision/recall
cards) as the proof-point.
